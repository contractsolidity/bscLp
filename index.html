  • What is Binance Chain and Binance Smart Chain? expand_more

    Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

    This dual-chain architecture will empower its users to build their decentralized apps and digital assets on one blockchain and take advantage of the fast trading to exchange on the other.



    Highlights:


    • EVM Compatible


    • Proof of Staked Authority


    • Cross-Chain Transfer


    • Block time ~3 seconds

  • Binance Chain Wallet What is it?expand_more

    Binance Chain Extension Wallet is a Crypto Wallet for Binance Chain, Binance Smart Chain. You can send and receive funds on Binance Chain, Binance Smart Chain and cross-chain transfers between both of Binance’s blockchains.


    Lately, Binance Chain Extension Wallet add support for Ethereum blockchain. It could enable access for distributed applications, or "Dapps" in your browser!

  • What is the BNB Reward Program?expand_more

    The BNB Rewards Program for Binance Smart Chain rewards liquidity pledges with an Expected annual return of 168-580%.


    • Completed mining Reward ( 10% of mining amount)


    • Expected liquidty mining Reward ( From the next day)


    • Recommended mining Reward (1 Step 2% , 2 Step 1%)
